## DAE v1.0

DAE v1.0 supports following effects:

+ **echo**:		echo effect
+ **stereo**: 	stereo effect
+ **tsm**:		time scale modification effect
+ **pm**:		pitch modification effect
+ **flanger**: 	flanger effect
+ **fade**:    	fade in, fade out, fade across effect
